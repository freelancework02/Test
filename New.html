Advice & solutions for debts like

Credit cards • Loans • Council tax • Buy-now-pay-later • Business debt

IVA

Individual Voluntary Arrangement

One affordable monthly payment, usually for 5–6 years. Remaining unsecured debt may be written off.

  • • Stops most interest and contact from creditors
  • • Protects essential assets
DMP

Debt Management Plan

Flexible informal plan with reduced payments negotiated on your behalf. Good if your situation may change.

  • • One payment distributed to creditors
  • • Can pause or adjust when needed
TD

Scottish Trust Deed

For Scottish residents. Legally binding agreement with fixed-term payments and a potential write-off at the end.

  • • Typically 4 years
  • • Stops most creditor action

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the big things people worry about.

Will a debt solution affect my partner?

Not usually. If the debts are solely in your name, your partner’s credit file shouldn’t be affected. Joint debts or shared finance can be.

Should I still pay my creditors?

Keep paying what you can until a solution is in place. We’ll help you prioritise essentials first.

Will entering a debt solution affect my credit rating?

Most do for up to six years. The trade-off is affordable payments and a path back to stability.

Fees?

Advice is free. Some solutions include fees—we’ll be upfront before you decide anything.
